The collectibles can also be traded right within the Sciverse Marketplace without relying on any third party marketplace.



How to contribute to the Sciverse collection? If you have a scientific work (published in 2000 or later) with an unpublished representative media (image/ video/ illustration), you can submit it to us here. We periodically review new works and add it to our collection. Follow us on twitter for updates!
